Tips for getting your remarkable insights

Welcome to 1self

And thanks for signing up!

Your data has so much to tell you and with 1self you’ll be able to discover the remarkable things that it says.

We’re building 1self to be the service that allows you to get maximum value from all of the data that’s collected about you. We haven’t been going long, so we’ve got a lot of building to do but we’re super-excited to have you on board at this early stage.

We want you to get the most from your data and have a great experience with 1self! Here are a few tips to get you started.

Integrations

1self works by linking with the different apps and websites that you use. We call these “integrations”. To get started take a look at our available integrations and link up the things that you use. If there’s one that you’d like that isn’t listed then let us know.

Your Card Stack

Once you’ve got some data flowing into 1self we’ll create cards for each bite-sized insight. Those cards will be placed on your card stack ready for you to swipe through and discover the remarkable things that you’ve done.

Help and Feedback

Something odd? Something to tell us? We’d love to hear from you. Head on over to our support area or drop us an email. We're here to help.

Thanks so much for your time — we hope you enjoy using 1self to discover your remarkable self.
